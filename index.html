Discover Bulgaria

In the land where east meets west, Bulgaria is home to a fascinating history and culture. Catch a glimpse of its rich heritage flashing by. Journey back in time and follow in the footsteps of ancient civilisations. Take in the stunning scenery and enjoy old traditions and a gentler pace of life. Discover a wealth of antiquities as inspiring as the sunsets.

Welcome to Bulgaria. An exciting trip awaits us. Situated on the Balkan peninsula, Bulgaria borders five countries - Greece and Turkey to the south, Macedonia and Serbia to the west, and Romania to the north.

Today, our journey starts high in the mountains where we can experience the beauty and uniqueness of Bulgaria. A place where deep glossy lakes relfect the sunlight from the early hours of the morning until twilight when crepuscular rays stream through cliffs towering above clouds.
